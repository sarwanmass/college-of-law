Course Offered

3 Years LLB Programme

Duration

3 Years (6 Semesters) | 18 weeks per semester

Eligibility

  • General Category: Graduation with 45% marks (44.5% and above)
  • OBC Category: Graduation with 42% marks (41.5% and above)
  • SC/ST Category: Graduation with 40% marks (39.5% and above)

Required Documents

  • SSLC/10th Mark Statement (original + 2 copies)
  • PUC/12th Mark Statement (original + 2 copies)
  • Degree Mark Statement (original + 2 copies)
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if non-Karnataka graduation)
  • 8 Passport-size Photographs

Our Faculty

Statutory Committees & Cells

Anti-Ragging Cell

Maintains a ragging-free environment on campus and ensures student welfare through awareness and preventive measures as per UGC guidelines.

Student Grievance Redressal Committee

Provides a fair and transparent mechanism for addressing student grievances and ensuring timely resolution of complaints.

National Service Scheme (NSS)

Engages students in community service and social development activities to bring positive change in society.

Legal Aid Cell

Provides free legal aid to underprivileged sections of society, enabling law students to serve as social engineers.

Human Rights Cell

Raises awareness about human rights and promotes dignity and justice among faculty, staff, and students.

SC/ST Cell

Ensures adequate representation and welfare of SC/ST students in accordance with government policies and guidelines.

Women's Grievance Cell

Addresses issues related to women students and staff, ensuring a safe and respectful campus environment.

Disciplinary Committee

Maintains discipline and order, investigates misconduct, and promotes a culture of responsibility and respect.

Moot Court

Provides practical training through moot court competitions, trial advocacy, and judgment writing exercises.

Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Ensures institutional quality and compliance with NAAC guidelines for continuous improvement.
